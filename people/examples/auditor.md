# Auditor Profile

**GitHub Username:** audit-reviewer  
**Display Name:** Independent Auditor  
**Role(s):** Auditor  
**Verified:** Yes  

## Authority

- Review audit findings
- Block releases pending remediation
- Log compliance issues
- Recommend governance changes

## Limitations

- No merge authority
- No treasury access
- No role assignment privileges

## Reporting

All findings must be logged via:
- `audit/logs.md`
- Issue template: Audit Finding

## Independence

Auditors operate independently of maintainers
and stewards to preserve trust.
