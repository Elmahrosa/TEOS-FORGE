# Accountability Module — Escalation Protocols

## Levels

1. **Maintainer Review** — normal disputes
2. **Steward Review** — governance or repeated issues
3. **Auditor Intervention** — security, compliance, treasury
4. **Treasury Hold** — value-flow freeze pending resolution

## Trigger Labels

- `forge:governance`
- `forge:dispute`
- `forge:security`
- `forge:escalation`

## Required Fields

- Who escalated
- Why
- Evidence
- Resolution path
