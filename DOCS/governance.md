## Origin Stewardship

TEOS Forge originated under the leadership of
**Elmahrosa International** as part of the **TEOS Egypt** initiative.

Elmahrosa International acts as the **originating steward** of the framework,
responsible for maintaining the reference governance model
and canonical documentation.

This stewardship role does not limit the sovereignty,
legal authority, or autonomy of adopting institutions.
