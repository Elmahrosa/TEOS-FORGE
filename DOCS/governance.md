# TEOS Forge Governance Constitution

This document is the authoritative governance framework for TEOS Forge.

---

## Principles

1. Contribution before authority
2. Verification before reward
3. Audit before trust
4. Escalation before conflict
5. Transparency over speed

---

## Roles

### Contributor
- Submits issues and PRs

### Steward
- Reviews governance proposals

### Maintainer
- Approves merges
- Enforces CI

### Auditor
- Logs findings
- Can block releases

---

## Proposal Lifecycle

1. Proposal opened
2. Review period
3. Decision logged
4. Implementation tracked

---

## Merge Rules

- Issue required
- CI required
- Review required
- Audit impact declared

---

## Escalation

1. Maintainer review
2. Steward review
3. Auditor intervention

---

## Value Flow

- No rewards without verification
- No payouts during disputes
- Audit overrides treasury actions
