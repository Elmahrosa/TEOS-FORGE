# Contributing to TEOS Forge

TEOS Forge is civic infrastructure.  
Contributions must meet a higher standard than typical open-source projects.

---

## Rules

- Every contribution must be linked to an Issue
- CI must pass
- Reviews are mandatory
- Governance and audit impact must be declared

Low-effort or speculative contributions will be rejected.

---

## Contribution Flow

1. Open Issue
2. Discussion & approval
3. Pull Request
4. CI + review
5. Merge with audit trace

---

## Recognition

Verified contributors may earn:
- Badges
- Roles
- Access rights
- Value-flow eligibility

---

Respect process over ego.  
Public good over personal gain.
