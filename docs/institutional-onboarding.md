# TEOS Forge — Institutional Onboarding Guide

This guide explains how institutions, governments, and enterprises
can adopt TEOS Forge safely and transparently.

---

## What Institutions Get

- Governed open-source collaboration
- Audit-ready contribution records
- Clear authority separation
- Value-flow discipline
- No vendor lock-in

---

## Adoption Model

Institutions may adopt TEOS Forge as:

1. Observer (read-only audit)
2. Contributor (governed participation)
3. Maintainer (enforcement authority)
4. Operator (white-label deployment)

---

## Onboarding Steps

### Step 1 — Governance Review
- Read `docs/governance.md`
- Review escalation and audit rules
- Confirm alignment with internal policy

### Step 2 — Repository Access
- Public repos require no approval
- Private forks follow local governance

### Step 3 — Role Assignment
- Roles are documented and reviewable
- No silent authority assignment

### Step 4 — Audit Alignment
- Map TEOS Forge audit checklist
- Align with internal compliance frameworks

### Step 5 — Value Flow (Optional)
- Enable treasury participation
- Require dual approval + audit clearance

---
