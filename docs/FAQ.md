# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is the relationship between Elmahrosa and TEOS Forge?
TEOS Forge is the engineering engine developed by **Elmahrosa International** under the vision of **Ayman Seif**. It is part of a broader mission to provide the world with Egyptian-originated Digital Public Infrastructure.

### Can TEOS Forge merge my code automatically?
**No.** By design, TEOS Forge is an observer and signaler. It provides the "Green Signal" for governance compliance, but the final action remains a human responsibility.

### Is TEOS Forge only for Egypt?
While it originates from Egypt, TEOS Forge is built for the global community. Its architecture is universal, allowing any nation to implement sovereign digital governance.

### How are the audit logs secured?
Every log entry generates a unique Audit ID and a cryptographic hash of the evidence. Any modification to a past log will break the hash chain, alerting auditors.
