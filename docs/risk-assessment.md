# TEOS Forge — Phase-to-Phase Risk Assessment

This document identifies risks introduced at each phase
and the controls that mitigate them.

---

## Phase 0 → Governance Foundation

**Risks**
- Ambiguous authority
- Informal decision-making

**Controls**
- Written constitution
- Role separation
- Escalation protocol

**Residual Risk:** Low

---

## Phase 1 → GitHub App Automation

**Risks**
- Over-automation
- Hidden enforcement logic

**Controls**
- Read/write limits
- No merge authority
- Human approvals required

**Residual Risk:** Low–Medium

---

## Phase 2 → Treasury Execution

**Risks**
- Misuse of funds
- Disputed payouts

**Controls**
- Dual approval
- Audit clearance
- Payout holds

**Residual Risk:** Medium

---

## Phase 3 → On-Chain Execution

**Risks**
- Smart contract rigidity
- Irreversible errors

**Controls**
- Off-chain governance gating
- Emergency pause
- Read-only transparency

**Residual Risk:** Medium

---

## Phase 4 → Federation

**Risks**
- Governance divergence
- Compliance mismatch

**Controls**
- Local sovereignty
- Audit compatibility
- Federation standards

**Residual Risk:** Medium–High

---

## Risk Posture Summary

TEOS Forge intentionally trades speed for trust.
All risks are **visible, documented, and governed**.
